Just a note on capacity and capacity calculation. The capacity calculation is a variable used by the state to determine if a zone is of reasonable size. It is unreasonable to think that every unit of housing to be built under this zone would be 1,000 square feet, or minus parking, or even get built at all. And that is why the measure of capacity is a variable and not a constant, and nor should it be treated as a constant. However, no matter what the capacity number is calculated, it will not include whatever housing is there already.
